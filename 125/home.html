BEANT COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Beant College of Engineering & Technology, Gurdaspur was established and promoted by the Govt. of Punjab in 1995 in order to boost the technical education in the border region of Punjab, and thus addressing the needs of educationally backward belt of the state. This is an autonomous Institution registered under the Societies Registration Act 1860. Its affairs are administered by a Board of Governors, a body which is constituted as per AICTE norms. The college campus is spread over 70 acres of Land which includes the instructional buildings, amenities, residential and other facilities. At present, we are offering technical education in six different engineering disciplines at graduation level and 3 engineering courses at diploma level. At master's level, we have M.Tech. regular program in Thermal Engineering and M.Sc. Physics and Mathematics. Apart from it, the institute is also a well recognized Regional Centre of Punjab Technical University, offering part time M. Tech. in various engineering disciplines. The college is fully equipped with state of art Labs in all the disciplines and having independent Computer Labs in each department beside the Central Computer Centre. The College is having independent Internet Connectivity through NIC-NKN (100Mbps bandwidth). The college is having Central Library with over 25,000 Books, E-Journals and A.C. Reading Hall.
